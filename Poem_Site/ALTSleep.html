The Tale of a long slumber

A battle that would be remembered for centuries, a clash that legends would be forged from. One evening, the Henyonme, a legendary being that hadn't graced this plane of existence in a millennium, made its return at the Golden Falls.

Guarding the falls was the Vicious Tiger, a guardian dedicated to its protection. The two engaged in a relentless struggle for days. Although the guardian's kin offered assistance, it proved insufficient as the Henyonme persisted in its unyielding attack.

The temples and structures that had held the denizens of the falls, gotten destroyed, leaving more chaos to brew. Suddenly, a monk appeared and cast an enchantment over the two beasts. With a graceful whirl of their arms, the adversaries succumbed to a slumber deeper than rest. Who is this mysterious savior, and what are their intentions?
